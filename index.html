About Me

Who Am I?

I am a highly motivated Fresh Graduate with a strong passion for Occupational Health and Safety (OHS/HSE). I have obtained General OHS certification from the Indonesian Ministry of Manpower (Kemnaker RI) and HSE Expert certification from the National Professional Certification Agency (BNSP).

With a solid understanding of risk assessment, safety inspections, hazard identification, and accident investigation, I am eager to contribute to a company's Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) management system. I am committed to ensuring workplace safety, minimizing hazards, and fostering a strong safety culture within organizations.
